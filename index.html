About Our Organisation

Child Education Focus Initiative (CEFI) is an indigenous, non-governmental, membership-based organization established in 2012 and formally registered as a national NGO in 2016. This was in response to the overwhelming marginalization, isolation, and abuse of the rights of children, youths, orphans from vulnerable communities, and their families.


Since its inception, CEFI has worked tirelessly to address the multifaceted challenges faced by these marginalized groups. The organization has implemented numerous programs aimed at improving access to quality education, healthcare, and social services. By collaborating with local communities and international partners, CEFI has been able to develop and sustain initiatives that empower children and young people, ensuring they have the necessary tools and opportunities to thrive. The focus on education is particularly crucial, as it not only provides immediate relief but also fosters long-term development and self-sufficiency within these communities.


Furthermore, CEFI places a strong emphasis on advocacy and awareness-raising to combat the systemic issues contributing to the marginalization and abuse of children and youths. Through workshops, campaigns, and policy dialogues, the organization seeks to influence positive change at both the local and national levels. By educating communities and policymakers about the importance of protecting the rights of vulnerable children and providing them with adequate support, CEFI aims to create a more inclusive and equitable society. The organization's dedication to its mission is reflected in its ongoing efforts to adapt and expand its programs to meet the evolving needs of the communities it serves.

MISSION

CEFI is a Christian, child protection, development, and advocacy organization dedicated to working with children, families, and communities to overcome poverty and other injustices. Our mission is to ensure a brighter future for vulnerable children through education, protection, and comprehensive community support.


VISION

To create a world where every child, youth, orphan, and vulnerable child, regardless of background or circumstance, has access to the tools, support, and opportunities necessary for educational success and personal growth.


OUR AIM

Our aim at the Child Education Focus Initiative (CEFI) is to empower vulnerable children, youths, and orphans by providing them with access to quality education, healthcare, and essential social services. We strive to create a supportive and inclusive environment where every child has the opportunity to reach their full potential, free from marginalization and abuse.


OUR FOCUS

At the Child Education Focus Initiative (CEFI), we concentrate our efforts on empowering vulnerable children, youths, and orphans from marginalized communities through comprehensive programs in key areas. Our focus includes providing access to quality education by building and supporting schools, offering scholarships, and creating safe learning environments. Advocacy for children's rights is central to our mission, as we work to combat abuse, neglect, and exploitation.

OUR CORE VALUES

Below are our core values:

Inclusivity

We believe in equal educational opportunities for all children. We strive to create environments where every child, regardless of background or ability, has access to quality education and the resources needed to succeed. By fostering inclusive practices, we ensure that all children feel valued and supported in their educational journey.

Empowerment

We strive to empower children, educators, and communities. By providing the tools, knowledge, and resources necessary for growth, we enable individuals to take charge of their own development. Our programmes focus on building confidence, enhancing skills, and promoting self-reliance, ensuring long-term positive impacts.

Collaboration

We work in partnership with families, schools, and community organisations. Collaboration is at the heart of our approach, as we believe that collective efforts yield the most sustainable results. By engaging all stakeholders, we create a supportive network that works together to address challenges and achieve common goals.

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of honesty and transparency in all our actions. Integrity is the foundation of our work, guiding every decision and interaction. By maintaining transparency and accountability, we build trust with our beneficiaries, partners, and supporters, ensuring that our impact is both genuine and lasting.

KEY OBJECTIVES

Below are our key objectives:

Support

Provide educational resources and support services to children and families in need. Our goal is to remove barriers to education by offering financial assistance, learning materials, and personalised support. We aim to create an environment where every child can thrive academically and socially, regardless of their circumstances.

Advocacy

Advocate for policies and practices that promote equitable education. We engage with policymakers, educators, and the broader community to champion reforms that ensure all children have access to high-quality education. Our advocacy efforts focus on creating systemic change that addresses educational disparities and promotes social justice.

Innovation

Develop and implement innovative educational programmes and strategies. We are committed to exploring new ideas and technologies that enhance the learning experience. Our innovative approaches are designed to address the unique challenges faced by our communities, ensuring that education remains relevant and effective in the modern world.

Awareness

Raise awareness about the importance of quality education for all children. We engage in public campaigns, community outreach, and educational workshops to highlight the critical role of education in personal and societal development. By fostering a culture that values education, we aim to inspire action and support for educational initiatives worldwide.

ORGANISATIONAL OBJECTIVES

1. To promote and improve access to quality education in vulnerable communities through construction of schools.
2. To promote and improve access to health and hygiene in vulnerable communities through construction of hospitals and staging healthy camps.
3. To improve the quality of life of vulnerable communities through construction of water sources and shelters for vulnerable.
4. To support orphans and vulnerable children through general welfare support, education and health sponsorship.
5. Poverty alleviation in marginalize community through skills training and providing start up kits to women an youth.
6. Advocating for people living with HIV/AIS, and fighting against sexual abuses, early marriages, and gender-based violence.
7. To put up income generating projects that will generate income to sustain the activities of the organisation.
8. To carry out any other activity that will be deemed to be important in achieving the organisation goals and objectives.


SOURCES OF FUNDING

1. Funders, members and well-wishers contributions in cash and in kind.
2. Community contributions mainly in terms of land and labour.
3. Donors from international relief organizations.
4. Agency funds from people who want charity work done on their behalf basing on the trust earned by the organization.
